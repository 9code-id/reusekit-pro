## Design Requirements:
- Clean typography dan spacing
- Mobile-first responsive design
- Each application consists of at least 100 views
- Prefix tiap apps pasti berbeda, idealnya terdiri dari 2-5 huruf saja ya,
  misalnya: car rental app > prefix-nya > cra\_
- Setiap aplikasi unik ya, jangan membuat section dimana sebenernya aplikasinya mirip2.
- Patuhi semua aturan di [copilot-instructions.md](../.github/copilot-instructions.md)
- Patuhin widget restrictions di [widget-restrictions.json](../.github/improvements/widget-restrictions.json)
```
"forbidden_widgets": [
    "SliverWidgets",
    "CustomScrollView",
    "ListTile",
    "drawer",
    "DefaultTabController",
    "TabBarView",
    "TabBar",
    "DropdownButton",
    "PageView",
    "SingleTickerProviderStateMixin"
],
```
- Jika perlu TabBar, gunakan [QTabBar](../lib/core/widget/scaffold/q_tab_bar.dart)
- Jika perlu BottomNavigationBar, gunakan [QBottomBar](../lib/core/widget/scaffold/q_bottom_bar.dart)

## Skip Apps Type
- Jangan buat aplikasi terkait video editing, video player, music dan lainnya
